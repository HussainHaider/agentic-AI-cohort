================================================================================
SMART EMAIL WRITER — USAGE EXAMPLES
================================================================================
Feature: write_email()  /  SmartEmailWriter.write()

Scenario: Sarah runs a small tech company called NovaTech Solutions.
She needs to communicate with clients, investors, and her team regularly.
================================================================================


────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
EXAMPLE 1 — Stakeholder update email (professional tone)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Input:
  purpose   : "Announce Q1 2026 results and outline Q2 growth strategy"
  recipient : "stakeholder"
  tone      : "professional"

Expected output format:
  Subject: NovaTech Solutions Q1 2026 Results & Q2 Strategy Overview

  Dear Valued Stakeholders,

  I am pleased to share NovaTech Solutions' Q1 2026 performance highlights.
  This quarter we achieved record revenue of $240,000, representing a 15%
  growth over Q4 2025, driven by strong demand in our cloud services division.

  Looking ahead to Q2 2026, we are investing in three strategic areas:
  expanding our enterprise client base, launching a self-service dashboard,
  and hiring two senior engineers to accelerate product delivery.

  We remain committed to transparency and will host a stakeholder call on
  June 5, 2026, to walk through the numbers and answer your questions.

  Thank you for your continued trust and support.

  Warm regards,
  Sarah Mitchell
  CEO, NovaTech Solutions

  Sarah Mitchell
