Feature: generate_report()  /  ReportGenerator.generate()

and quarterly planning sessions at NovaTech Solutions.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
EXAMPLE 1 — Q1 Sales Report
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Input:
  report_type : "sales"
  data        : "Jan:50000, Feb:65000, Mar:72000"
  period      : "Q1 2026"

Expected output format:
  ============================================
  Q1 2026 SALES REPORT
  ============================================
  Date: March 31, 2026

  EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
  NovaTech Solutions delivered strong Q1 2026 performance with total revenue of
  $187,000, a 15.5% increase over Q4 2025. March was the best month, recording
  $72,000 — a 44% jump from January.

  KEY METRICS
  - Total Q1 Revenue:    $187,000
  - Monthly Average:     $62,333
  - Best Month:          March ($72,000)
  - Month-over-Month Growth (Jan→Mar): +44%
  - Q1 vs Q4 2025 Growth: +15.5%

  ANALYSIS
  The upward trend across all three months suggests strong pipeline momentum.
  January's $50,000 reflects post-holiday seasonality, while February and March
  show accelerating deal closures driven by the enterprise segment...

  RECOMMENDATIONS
  1. Double down on enterprise outreach — it drove 60% of Q1 revenue.
  2. Investigate January's slower pace and add promotions to counter seasonality.
  3. Set a Q2 target of $220,000 based on current growth trajectory.
  ============================================

EXAMPLE 4 — Convenience Wrappers
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Shorthand methods available:
  generator.generate_sales_report(data, period)
  generator.generate_revenue_report(data, period)
  generator.generate_performance_report(data, period)

These are equivalent to calling generator.generate('sales' | 'revenue' | 'performance', data, period).


================================================================================
QUICK REFERENCE — API CALL
================================================================================

  from business_assistant.features.report_generator import ReportGenerator

  generator = ReportGenerator()

  # Direct API call
  report = generator.generate(
      report_type="sales",
      data="Jan:50000, Feb:65000, Mar:72000",
      period="Q1 2026"
  )
  print(report)

  # Convenience wrapper
  report = generator.generate_sales_report("50000,65000,72000", "Q1 2026")

  # Interactive (prompts user for all inputs)
  generator.run()
